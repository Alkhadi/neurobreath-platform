Before you hit reset

Check the basics

Clear 60 seconds, silence alerts, and notice three physical cues (jaw, shoulders, hands). This quick audit sets you up for the deeper hero plan below.

  • Pick one breathing pattern you can complete right now.
  • Tell someone nearby you‚Äôre taking a micro break.
  • Stand or sit tall so nasal breathing stays open.

What you may notice

  • Racing thoughts, muscle tension, shallow breathing
  • Irritability, poor focus, trouble winding down

Rapid loop

Complete in < 4 min
  1. Silence alerts, plant both feet, start SOS-60 or Box Breathing.
  2. On each exhale, scan for tension in jaw, hands, shoulders and release.
  3. Stand, walk slowly for 45 seconds, then commit to the next priority.

Share this plan with a colleague or partner so resets become normalised.
