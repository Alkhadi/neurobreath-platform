Micro-rest tools you can actually use

You may not get spa days or long holidays. What you might get are 3â€“10 minute pockets. Used well, these can still make a difference.

  • One slow drink (tea, water) without doing anything else.
  • Two or three slow breaths before answering a difficult question.
  • Looking out of a window or at a tree for 60 seconds between tasks.
  • Five minutes of gentle stretching before bed or after a meltdown.
