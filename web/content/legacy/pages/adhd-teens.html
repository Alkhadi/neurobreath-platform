Screens, sleep and social media

Short-form video, gaming and endless scrolling are built to grab attention. That is not a personal failure â€” it is persuasive design. The aim is to choose when to dive in and when to pause.

  • Park your phone in another room or upside down when you need a break. Out of sight helps.
  • Follow creators who leave you feeling calmer, smarter or kinder to yourself.
  • Notice what happens to your mood after certain apps. If it is consistently worse, that is data.
  • Try to pair late-night scrolling with a wind-down alarm that nudges you towards sleep.
