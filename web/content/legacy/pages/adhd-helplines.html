Sometimes ADHD is only part of the picture. Stress, anxiety, low mood or crisis moments can appear. This page points you towards people and services who can listen and, where needed, act.

Before you scroll

This website cannot see your individual situation. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, treat it as an emergency.

  • UK: Call 999 or go to your nearest A&E.
  • Outside the UK, use your local emergency number or hospital.

For non-emergency but urgent mental health support in the UK, you can contact NHS 111 (option 2 in some areas) or your local crisis team if you already have their details.

A small pause before contacting someone
