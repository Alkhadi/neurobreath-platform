New stages: college, apprenticeships, work

Moving into further education, training or work often exposes ADHD more clearly: deadlines widen, structure drops away and you are expected to self-organise everything.

  • Coursework and placements may be flexible but also less supervised.
  • Money, bills and travel planning now sit on your shoulders.
  • People may expect you to â€œjust knowâ€ how to plan, prioritise and show up.

None of this means you are incapable. It means you need tools, not shame.
